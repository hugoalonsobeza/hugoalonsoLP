01 — About

Websites that sell.
Brands that get noticed.

I'm Hugo. I'm finishing a Double Degree in Business Administration + Computer Engineering at the University of Deusto, and I work as a freelancer helping brands, small businesses and personal projects build a digital presence that lives up to what they actually offer.

I design and build websites that are clean, fast and built to convert. And I pair them with the parts most people don't want to look at: SEO, content, analytics, campaigns. When all of that talks to each other, your website stops being a digital business card and starts being a sales channel.

That's my edge: I'm not just a designer, just a developer, or just a marketer. I think about the project through a business lens, build it like an engineer and scale it through digital marketing. Three lenses, one project.

I understand margins, funnels and value props. Before writing any code, I know what's actually supposed to move.

/ Engineering
